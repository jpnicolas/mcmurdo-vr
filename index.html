Byrd Polar & Climate Research Center

Around McMurdo Station, Antarctica

By Julien Nicolas

For most U.S. scientists doing Antarctic field work, McMurdo Station is the first stop on the way to the Ice. This app lets you take a virtual reality tour around McMurdo and discover what these scientists see when they step out of the plane.

Take a virtual reality tour around McMurdo Station, Antarctica.

Instructions

  • Hold the phone horizontally, tilt and rotate to navigate. You may want to stand up!
  • Tap to start and stop movement.

Instructions

  • Use button to start and stop tour
  • Press Go and place phone in virtual reality device once split screen is displayed.

Instructions

  • W, A, S, D move forward, backwards, left and right.
  • Q, E keys roll left and right.
  • R, F keys move up and down.
  • Hold shift to speed up.
